## Logo

### Primary Mark (Dark Background)
**File:** `TESSRYX-logo-primary-dark.png` (1536x1024px)  
**Primary Use:** All dark backgrounds (preferred)

The TESSRYX logo features an impossible geometric structure representing constraint solving and dependency intelligence. A golden path threads through the interlocking design, symbolizing the discovery of valid solutions through complexity. The bright central star represents verification, trust, and evidence-backed proof.

---

## Logo Symbolism

**Visual Elements & Meaning:**

1. **Impossible Geometry Structure**
   - Represents: Constraint solving (making the impossible possible, or proving it's impossible)
   - Message: Complex problems require sophisticated solutions
   - Feeling: Precision, intelligence, technical sophistication

2. **Golden Path**
   - Represents: Valid solution threading through constraints
   - Message: Proof-carrying plans, verified dependencies
   - Feeling: Confidence, certainty, guidance through complexity

3. **Interlocking Pieces**
   - Represents: Interconnected dependencies, relationships
   - Message: Everything is connected, understanding requires seeing the whole system
   - Feeling: Comprehensive, systematic, holistic

4. **Central Verification Star**
   - Represents: Trust, evidence, formal proof, validation
   - Message: Truth at the core, verification not approximation
   - Feeling: Trustworthy, authoritative, reliable

5. **Blue + Silver + Gold Color Palette**
   - Blue: Technical precision, trust, infrastructure
   - Silver: Sophistication, engineering excellence
   - Gold: Premium value, verified truth, optimal paths
   - Overall: Premium infrastructure software aesthetic

## Usage Guidelines

### Do
- ✅ Use on dark backgrounds
- ✅ Maintain clear space around logo
- ✅ Scale proportionally
- ✅ Use for GitHub, documentation, presentations

### Don't
- ❌ Distort or stretch
- ❌ Change colors
- ❌ Add effects (shadows, glows)
- ❌ Place on busy backgrounds
- ❌ Rotate or tilt
