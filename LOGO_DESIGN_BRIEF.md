## What TESSRYX Is

TESSRYX is a universal dependency intelligence infrastructure that transforms dependency mapping from visualization into verification. It's the "Git for dependencies" meets "constraint solver for reality."

## Core Concept

Most tools show you dependency graphs (visualization). TESSRYX proves they're valid or explains why they're impossible (verification). It's the difference between drawing a blueprint and engineering a proof.

- **Universal Core:** Works across software, infrastructure, manufacturing, construction
